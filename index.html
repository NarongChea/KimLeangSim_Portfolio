About Me

Driven by Purpose,
Defined by Care

I am Chili Leang, a 25-year-old pharmacist based in Phnom Penh, Cambodia. With a Bachelor's degree in Pharmacy from IU International University and studies at Paññāsāstra University, I bring a well-rounded academic and clinical foundation to every patient interaction.

Beyond my professional work, I volunteer with the medical team of H.E. Hun Many — contributing to community health initiatives and grassroots healthcare access across Cambodia. I believe in the power of pharmacy to transform lives.

My multilingual background — fluent in Khmer, with intermediate English and basic Chinese — enables me to connect with diverse patients and healthcare teams across different communities.
